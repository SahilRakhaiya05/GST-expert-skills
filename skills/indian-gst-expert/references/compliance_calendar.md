## 1. Return Types & Eligibility

| Return | Description | Who Must File |
|---|---|---|
| **GSTR-1** | Outward supplies (sales details) | All regular taxpayers |
| **GSTR-3B** | Summary return — sales, ITC, tax paid | All regular taxpayers |
| **IFF** | Invoice Furnishing Facility (optional monthly B2B upload for QRMP) | QRMP taxpayers only |
| **PMT-06** | Monthly GST payment challan for QRMP | QRMP taxpayers only |
| **GSTR-4** | Annual return for composition dealers | Composition scheme dealers |
| **CMP-08** | Quarterly self-assessed tax payment for composition dealers | Composition scheme dealers |
| **GSTR-5** | Return for non-resident taxable persons | Non-resident taxpayers |
| **GSTR-6** | Input Service Distributor return | ISD registrants |
| **GSTR-7** | TDS return | TDS deductors under GST |
| **GSTR-8** | TCS return | E-commerce operators |
| **GSTR-9** | Annual return | Regular taxpayers with AATO > ₹2 Crore |
| **GSTR-9C** | Reconciliation statement (CA-certified) | Taxpayers with AATO > ₹5 Crore |
| **GSTR-2B** | Auto-populated ITC statement | For reference (no filing needed) |

## 2. Monthly Filing Calendar

For taxpayers with **AATO > ₹5 Crore** (mandatory monthly filers):

| Date | Return | Period |
|---|---|---|
| **10th** | GSTR-7 (TDS), GSTR-8 (TCS) | Previous month |
| **11th** | GSTR-1 | Previous month |
| **13th** | GSTR-5, GSTR-6 | Previous month |
| **20th** | GSTR-3B, GSTR-5A | Previous month |

### Example: April 2026 (for March 2026 transactions)
- 10 Apr 2026 — GSTR-7, GSTR-8
- 11 Apr 2026 — GSTR-1
- 13 Apr 2026 — GSTR-5, GSTR-6
- 20 Apr 2026 — GSTR-3B

## 3. Quarterly Filing (QRMP Scheme)

For taxpayers with **AATO ≤ ₹5 Crore** who opt into QRMP:

| Action | Due Date |
|---|---|
| **GSTR-1** (quarterly) | 13th of month after quarter end |
| **GSTR-3B** (quarterly) | 22nd or 24th of month after quarter (state-dependent) |
| **PMT-06** (monthly tax payment) | 25th of each month (M1, M2 of quarter) |
| **IFF** (optional B2B upload, months 1 & 2) | 13th of the following month |

### QRMP Quarter Deadlines

| Quarter | GSTR-1 Due | GSTR-3B Due (Cat-X) | GSTR-3B Due (Cat-Y) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q1: Apr–Jun 2026 | 13 Jul 2026 | 22 Jul 2026 | 24 Jul 2026 |
| Q2: Jul–Sep 2026 | 13 Oct 2026 | 22 Oct 2026 | 24 Oct 2026 |
| Q3: Oct–Dec 2026 | 13 Jan 2027 | 22 Jan 2027 | 24 Jan 2027 |
| Q4: Jan–Mar 2027 | 13 Apr 2027 | 22 Apr 2027 | 24 Apr 2027 |

### PMT-06 Monthly Payment (QRMP, FY 2026-27)

| Month | PMT-06 Due |
|---|---|
| Apr 2026 | 25 Apr 2026 |
| May 2026 | 25 May 2026 |
| Jun 2026 | Paid via GSTR-3B (25 Jun skip — file GSTR-3B by 22/24 Jul) |
| Jul 2026 | 25 Jul 2026 |
| ... | (continues pattern) |

> **Rule:** Pay PMT-06 in months 1 and 2 of each quarter. In month 3, payment is included in GSTR-3B.

## 4. Annual Returns

| Return | Who | Due Date | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **GSTR-9** | Regular taxpayers, AATO > ₹2 Crore | **31 December** | For FY 2025-26: 31 Dec 2026. Voluntary for ≤ ₹2 Crore. |
| **GSTR-9C** | AATO > ₹5 Crore | **31 December** | Self-certified reconciliation (CA not required since FY 2020-21 changes) |
| **GSTR-4** | Composition dealers | **30 April** | For FY 2025-26: 30 Apr 2026 |

> **New from Jan 2026:** Automatic late fees apply to GSTR-9 from the day after the due date (previously manual enforcement).

> **3-Year Rule (from Dec 2025):** GST returns for periods older than 3 years from their original due date **cannot be filed**. Returns for FY 2022-23 and earlier are permanently blocked. Clear all pending filings immediately.

## 5. Late Fee Structure

### GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B Late Fees

| Return Type | Late Fee per Day | Cap |
|---|---|---|
| Nil return (no transactions) | ₹20/day (₹10 CGST + ₹10 SGST) | ₹500 per return |
| Return with transactions | ₹50/day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) | ₹5,000 per return |

### GSTR-9 and GSTR-9C Late Fees (Annual Returns)

| Turnover | Late Fee per Day | Maximum Cap |
|---|---|---|
| Up to ₹5 Crore | ₹50/day | 0.04% of turnover in state |
| ₹5–20 Crore | ₹100/day | 0.04% of turnover in state |
| Above ₹20 Crore | ₹200/day | 0.25% of turnover in state |

### Composition Scheme Late Fees (CMP-08)

| Return | Late Fee per Day | Cap |
|---|---|---|
| CMP-08 | ₹50/day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) | ₹2,000 per return |

## 6. Interest on Late Tax Payment

| Situation | Interest Rate |
|---|---|
| Late payment of output tax liability | **18% per annum** |
| Undue/excess ITC claimed (fraudulent) | **24% per annum** |
| Wrongly availed ITC (on reversal) | **18% per annum** |

> Interest is computed on a **daily basis** from the day after the due date until actual payment.

**Formula:** Interest = Tax Amount × (18/100) × (Number of days delayed / 365)

## 8. Composition Scheme Summary

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Eligibility (Goods) | AATO ≤ ₹1.5 Crore (₹75 Lakh in some special states) |
| Eligibility (Services) | AATO ≤ ₹50 Lakh (service-only providers: 6% GST) |
| Rate (Manufacturers) | 1% of turnover (0.5% CGST + 0.5% SGST) |
| Rate (Traders) | 1% of turnover |
| Rate (Restaurants) | 5% of turnover |
| Rate (Service providers) | 6% of turnover |
| ITC | Not available (cannot charge GST on invoices to buyers) |
| Interstate supplies | Not permitted (except services up to ₹5 Lakh) |
| Returns | CMP-08 (quarterly) + GSTR-4 (annual) |
| E-invoicing | Exempt |

## 9. Key 2026 New Rules Summary

| Rule | Effective | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **ITC Hard Block** | Jan 1, 2026 | GSTR-3B blocked if RCM liabilities unpaid or credit ledger negative |
| **Mandatory IMS** (Invoice Management System) | Jan 1, 2026 | Recipients must action supplier invoices via IMS; importers must include BoE |
| **Auto late fees on GSTR-9** | Jan 1, 2026 | System auto-imposes late fees from day 1 |
| **Post-sale discount ITC reversal** | Apr 1, 2026 | No prior agreement needed; buyer must reverse proportionate ITC via IMS |
| **Export refund minimum removed** | Apr 1, 2026 | ₹1,000 minimum IGST export refund threshold abolished |
| **Bank account validation** | 2026 | Invalid bank details cause auto-suspension of GST registration |
| **3-year return bar** | Dec 2025 | Returns for FY 2022-23 and earlier permanently blocked |
| **MFA mandatory** | 2026 | Multi-factor authentication required for GST portal login |
